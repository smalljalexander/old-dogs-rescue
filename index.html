WHERE OLD DOGS GET A NEW LIFE

At Old Dogs Rescue we go through local animal shelters to find old dogs and provide them with a comfortable home environment for their latest years.

watch the video
WHY

We believe in a normal animal shelter environment is not the best enviroment for older dogs. We want to provide an enviroment where the dogs go to a peaceful home enviroment to live out the rest of their years.
